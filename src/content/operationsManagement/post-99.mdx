breakeven analysis 


Let's say you want to open a company. 

The money you invest in it to open it is called fixed costs (c<sub>f</sub>.)

Cost of each coffee (unit variable cost) : $5 

You sell the coffee for: $13

There is a difference between v and v<sup>*</sup>. 

v means number of units you want to sell.   
 v<sup>*</sup> means number of units required to sell in order to make $0 in profit (or in order to cover your fixed costs.)
