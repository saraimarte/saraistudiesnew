**1. Trading Comparables Analysis:** what we use when valuing a minority stake (some (but not majority) shares of stock) of a firm based on how similar publicly traded companies are currently priced.  

**2. Precedent Transactions Analysis:** what we use when valuing all the shares (Equity Value) of a firm based on past M&A (Mergers & Acquisitions) deals.

In Trading Comps: You look for comparable companies that are currently trading on the public stock market to see what everyday investors are paying for their shares, giving you the value of a minority stake.

In Precedent Transactions: You look for comparable companies that were recently bought in M&A deals to see what acquirers actually paid to take over the whole business, giving you the value of a majority stake.
