**Outline of Steps**

**Step 1:** Define your Null Links. Every node has a left and right link. Some of those links have nodes, some don't so those links are null. Write **N** for those nodes who's links have no nodes. 

**Step 2:** Label each vertex/node with 1(on the left), 2(in between links) and 3 (on the right).  

**Step 3:** Start at the root and go to the left no matter what traversal method your doing. Always continue path to the next node directly connected to it **without crossing links**. Depending on your method, write the key(the number in the node) when you pass the number 1, 2, or 3 label associated with that node.   

</Rulebox>
