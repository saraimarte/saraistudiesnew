# Integrity and Character 

altuism is helping other people 

How would each philosophy approach a white lie? 

Utiliatariam| the greatest good for the greatest amount of people (greatest good for greatest numbers)
Deontoloogy | do onto others as they do onto you  golden rule ends dont justify the means 
Virtue Ethics | what would a virtuous/good person do?  


Utiliatariam|  if it does more good than harm   
Deontoloogy |  you wouldnt want to be lied to so dont lie  
Virtue Ethics| it depends - if their virtues are honesty and fairness they wouldnt lie if they care for people (u) as their virtue then they would lie if it helps. Its sort of up to the persons virtues.
