<RuleBox align = "left">
**Def.** A **Federated System** is a type of architecture where different independent entities work together as a single unit.
</RuleBox>

<RuleBox align = "left">
**Def.** **Internet Service Providers** are entities that provide internet service (like Optimum, Verizon, etc.)
</RuleBox>

<RuleBox align = "left">
**Def.** **Interoperability** is the ability of different systems, devices, or applications to connect, communicate, and exchange data securely and effectively.
Interoperability is the Internet's most important goal.
</RuleBox>
