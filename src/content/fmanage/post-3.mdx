What does time have to do with money?

If you invest in a stock, you hope that the share price goes up over time. 

If you are considering taking out a loan, you would want to know how much that loan would be worth in the future since inflation will increase the cost of the loan.  

In this lesson we will learn how to manipulate money through time.
