# Balance of Payments 

Credit => sell something from the U.S. overseas and we receive capital for that. so that gets converted to U.S. dollars.  

Debit => we pay for product made outside and bring it to the U.S. This requires our dollars coming out so we're increasing our supply of dollars. Because by paying for that product we are putting out dollars.
